## QQQ Cross-Validation

Applying the same strategies to QQQ (Nasdaq 100 ETF) yielded approximately 45% higher returns than SPY, consistent with QQQ's higher volatility creating larger gaps and stronger reversion.

## 2025 Forward Test Results

Out-of-sample testing on January–February 2025 data showed:
- **All top strategies remained profitable**
- Average Sharpe ratio improved by 94% compared to the full backtest period
- Win rates held steady within 2–3% of historical averages

## Key Observations

1. **Volatility regime filtering is the single most impactful improvement** — VIX 13–22 window consistently outperforms
2. **Combining 2+ independent filters compounds edge** without significant trade reduction
3. **Gap & Go (momentum) underperforms mean reversion** on SPY but shows promise on higher-beta instruments
4. **Machine learning models match but do not significantly exceed** well-tuned rule-based strategies
5. **Day-of-week effects exist** — Fridays consistently underperform
